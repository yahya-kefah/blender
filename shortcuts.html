Welcome to blender 2.8 shortcuts

  • G to grap in all axises
  • GX to grap in x axis
  • GY to grap in y axis
  • GY to grap in y axis
  • E to extrude in the object rotation
  • Ez to extrude in the object z axis
  • Ex to extrude in the object x axis
  • Ey to extrude in the object y axis
  • s to scale in all axises
  • sx to scale in x axis
  • sy to scale in y axis
  • sz to scale in z axis
  • ctrl Z to undo
  • shift A to add a mesh
